We are a family business. We work with family and our employees feel like family. The same thought is echoed to the patients under our care. As a family practice, we take care of every member of the family throughout all stages of life.


Stewart and Stewart, A Medical Corporation DBA Stewart Medical Group was established by Dr. James R. Stewart and his brother Dr. John T. Stewart in 1981. Both Dr. James and Dr. John were born and raised in Alhambra and eventually took over the practice of two brothers, Dr. O.L. Putler and Dr. S.D. Putler. The previous owners were the family physicians for both Dr. James and Dr. John growing up.

Stewart Medical Group has developed into one of the larger private practices in the San Gabriel Valley serving a patient population of all ages. The professional staff of Stewart Medical Group consists of three full time physicians and two full time PA-C; Dr. James R. Stewart, his brother and partner; Dr. John T. Stewart; long time family friend, Dr. Anita Mercado; Dick Chuang, PA-C, and Sonya Tang, PA-C. Stewart Medical Group maintains a supportive staff of 25 employees.

CARDIOLOGY


Cardiologists specialize in diagnosing and treating diseases of the cardiovascular system. If a patient has symptoms of a heart condition, their physician may refer them to a cardiologist. Symptoms that can indicate a heart problem include shortness of breath, dizziness, chest pains, changes in heart rate or rhythm, and high blood pressure. ACUPUNCTURE


Whether it is acute or chronic pain in your head, neck, shoulders, elbows, wrists, waist, hips, legs, knees or other places, you should immediately seek medical attention and treatment. Acupuncture is effective in eliminating pain and muscle-contraction as it promotes local blood circulation, which in turn supplies oxygen and nutrients to affected muscles. It helps lessen inflammation and improve both the circulatory and self-immune systems. Acupuncture is not only effective for muscle strain and sprain, but also for neurological disorders such as sciatica, paralysis related to stroke, facial palsy, insomnia and work pressure.
